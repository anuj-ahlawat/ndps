# **App Name**: NDPS Hub

## Core Features:

- School Overview: Display comprehensive information about New Delhi Public School (NDPS), including its history, mission, and values.
- Academic Programs: Showcase academic programs offered by NDPS, with detailed curriculum information and faculty profiles.
- Visual Gallery: Provide a gallery of school photos and videos showing campus life, events, and student activities.
- News and Events: Integrate a news section featuring announcements, school events, and student achievements.
- Contact Information: Offer a contact form and important contact information for inquiries, admissions, and general communication.
- AI Chatbot: Implement an AI-powered virtual assistant to answer frequently asked questions about NDPS. The virtual assistant should use a tool for determining when its responses should be formal and geared toward parents, or informal and geared toward students.
- External Links: Link to external resources like the school's social media accounts, parent portal, and online learning platforms.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#1E3A8A) to evoke trust, stability, and intelligence, reflecting the school's educational mission.
- Background color: Light blue (#D6E5FA), a desaturated variant of the primary color, for a clean, professional backdrop.
- Accent color: Vibrant yellow-orange (#F59E0B), analogous to blue, used for CTAs and highlights to draw attention.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' serif, giving an elegant and fashionable high-end feel to titles and headers.
- Body font: 'PT Sans' sans-serif for readability, paired with Playfair for a balance of modernity and readability in content sections.
- Use clean, modern icons to represent different sections and functions on the website.
- Implement a grid-based layout with clear sections and intuitive navigation.
- Incorporate subtle animations for page transitions and interactive elements to enhance user experience.
